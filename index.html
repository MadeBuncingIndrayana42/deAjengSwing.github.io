Our Story

Born from a deep love for Palembang's cultural heritage, DeAjeng Sewing Craft combines the beauty of songket and jumputan fabrics with modern design to create bags that are not just functional, but also works of art that tell the story of Palembang's rich culture.

Each bag we create is the result of meticulous craftsmanship and dedication from local artisans who have mastered traditional techniques for decades. With every stitch, we are committed to preserving cultural heritage while bringing designs that resonate with modern lifestyles.
